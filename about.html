About Me

Howdy! I'm Ramesh Parthiban

My love of wild started from a young age, learning to respect and care for wildlife was instilled into me by my mother,who took me to nearby woods and places where wildlife was as a small child

My creative and emotional attachment to nature is at the very heart of each image, Creating a unique and artistic refection of m time in the field.it is m intention to use these reflections of the natural world to bring people's awareness of what beautiful wildlife we have on our doorstep and all around us. Encounters with nature put into place the platform for my journey to become a wildlife photographer.
